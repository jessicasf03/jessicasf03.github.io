Shawn Mendes The Skinny Legend

Shawn Mendes is a Canadian singer/song-writer who has taken off with his career. This webpage will include information about his career and his personal achievements.

His Career:

Shawn first started posting videos of himself singing on an app called Vine. This gained him some popularity, which led him to join a group called Magcon. Magcon would tour around the U.S and this increased his fame. Shortly after, Mendes signed to Island Records and teamed up with Andrew Gertler who would play the role of being his manager. He then went on tour as the opening act for artists like Austin Mahone and Taylor Swift. His first album released in 2014, which he performed live on his own tour. Shawn Mendes as continued to make music, which includes his second album in 2016 and his newest album dropped in 2019. Shawn Mendes is currently on his world tour for his third album. Mendes has multiple hit singles and has collaborated with artists like Khalid, Camila Cabello, and Julia Michaels.
